High-quality lead generation campaigns cost more but provide better ROI.
Clients often negotiate pricing, but emphasizing ROI and long-term benefits improves deal success.

Urgency tactics like limited-time offers can push clients to close deals faster.
Most clients prefer flexible pricing or milestone-based payments.

Discounts should be used strategically, not immediately.
Retainer-based contracts (6 to 12 months) are highly preferred for stability, and offering a 10% discount on long-term commitments is a standard concession.

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) ad setup fees typically range from ₹5,000 to ₹15,000, with monthly management fees set at 10-15% of the total ad spend.
Negotiators who establish high initial anchors (e.g. quoting higher first) achieve higher final contract values.
Using the 'Feel, Felt, Found' technique defuses pricing objections effectively (e.g., 'other clients felt the same, but they found our ROI covered the cost').
Providing a multi-tiered proposal (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold packages) reduces client hesitation and prevents them from shopping around.
Highlighting case studies of a 3x to 5x ROI on similar ad campaigns makes high setup fees significantly more acceptable to skeptical buyers.
Asking diagnostic open-ended questions like 'What is your target customer acquisition cost?' shifts the conversation from expense to growth investment.
